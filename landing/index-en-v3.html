FAQ

Questions, answered.

When will Quill Cloud be available?

We're working on it now. Joining the waitlist puts you in line for beta access — we'll reach out to early members before the public launch.

What do I get as an early member?

Beta access before the public launch, plus the chance to give direct feedback that shapes the product. We'll send you an invite link when your spot is ready.

Which Mac apps does Quill work in?

Nearly all of them — Mail, Notes, Pages, Messages, Slack, Safari, Chrome, and more. Quill reads your selected text and rewrites it in place, or shows the result in a panel for read-only apps like PDFs.

Why does Quill need Accessibility permission?

It's how Quill reads your selected text and replaces it in place. You grant it once in System Settings — it takes about 10 seconds. No other data is accessed.

Is it really free to join the waitlist?

Yes — joining the waitlist is completely free. No credit card, no commitment. We'll share pricing details before beta access opens.

Was the headline demo calling an API?

No — the demo on this page is scripted to show the interaction. The real app sends your selection to the AI model you configure.
